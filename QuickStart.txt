Q: Why is bold.inc is not found ?
A: Make a new variable in Delphi. Go to menu Tools/Options/Environment Variables.
   Make a new variable BoldDelphi and set it to folder for Bolds source. Ex: C:\BoldForDelphi
   Note no ending backslash. The variable is used in project options.
